## 📲 1. Download the App

Download the official “geteduroam” app from the Google Play Store:

👉 [geteduroam on Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.eduroam.geteduroam)

---

## 🔎 2. Select Your University

- Open the app after installation
- Enter **“Ulm University”** in the search field and select the corresponding entry

---

## 🔐 3. Log In with Your kiz Account

- **Username:**
  `firstname.lastname@uni-ulm.de`
- **Password:**
  Your **kiz password** (the same one used for services like Moodle)

---

## ✅ 4. Confirm the Connection

- Tap **“Allow”** when prompted to install the profile
- Then tap **“Connect”**

Your device will now automatically connect to eduroam®.

---

## 👁️ 5. Verify the Connection

Once you are within the university’s Wi-Fi range, you can check if your device is successfully connected to eduroam®:

- At the bottom of the Ulmiversity site, a notification will appear stating "_Connected to eduroam®_."

---

## 📎 Notes

- If you change your password, you must repeat the setup process
